Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

At Emora AI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and website.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may collect your email address, name, and profile picture when you create an account.

Mood and Emotional Data: To provide our services, we collect the mood logs, notes, and activity data you input into the app. This is the core of your experience with Emora AI.

AI Data: Our AI models process your inputs to provide insights. While we use this data for personalized analysis, we strive to anonymize data used for general model training.

Usage Data: We automatically collect information on how you interact with our services, including device information, IP address, and app usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Emora AI experience.
  • To generate personalized emotional insights and pattern recognition.
  • To process transactions and manage your subscription.
  • To communicate with you about updates, features, and support.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our services.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your emotional data at rest and during transmission. Your data is stored on secure servers with limited access to authorized personnel only.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal or emotional data to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes or with service providers who assist in our operations (e.g., payment processors, hosting services).

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You can export your mood data or request permanent account deletion via the app settings or by contacting our support team.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at privacy@emora.ai.
