Our Mission

We believe that mental well-being should be accessible, intuitive, and data-driven.

The Emora Story

Emora was founded by a team of psychologists and designers who noticed a gap in how people track their emotional health. Traditional journals are too time-consuming, and most apps are too clinical.

We built Emora to be a companion that feels like a friend, helping you uncover patterns you never knew existed.
