Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Emora.

01 General

What is Emora AI?

+
Emora AI is an advanced emotional health companion that uses artificial intelligence to help you track, understand, and improve your mental well-being through pattern recognition and personalized insights.

How does the mood tracking work?

+
You can log your mood using emojis, text descriptions, or even voice notes. Our AI analyzes your entries, looking for correlations between your activities, sleep, and emotional states.

Is Emora AI free to use?

+
Yes, Emora AI offers a free version with core tracking features. We also offer a Premium subscription for advanced AI insights, unlimited voice logs, and data exporting features.

02 Privacy & Security

Is my personal data safe?

+
Absolutely. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption for all stored data. Your emotional logs are private and encrypted at rest and in transit.

Who can see my mood entries?

+
Only you. Emora AI employees cannot access your private logs. The AI processing happens securely, and only aggregated, anonymized data is used for improving the system.

How do you protect my privacy from hackers?

+
We implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) options, session monitoring, and regular security audits to ensure your data remains protected against unauthorized access.

03 Features & Insights

What are "AI Patterns"?

+
AI Patterns are hidden correlations our system finds in your data—like how your mood might consistently improve after light exercise or dip during rainy weather.

Can I log my mood via voice?

+
Yes! Our voice recognition feature allows you to speak freely about your day, and the AI will transcribe and analyze the emotional sentiment for you.

Can I export my data for my therapist?

+
Definitely. You can generate professional PDF reports or CSV exports of your mood trends to share with your healthcare providers.

04 Billing & Pricing

How do I manage my subscription?

+
You can manage your billing, change plans, or cancel anytime through the "Manage Subscription" section in the app settings or on our website.

Is there a refund policy?

+
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all new Premium subscriptions if you're not completely satisfied with the features.

Do you offer student discounts?

+
Yes! We believe mental health tools should be accessible. Students can get 50% off Emora Premium by verifying their student ID.

05 Technical & Support

Which devices does Emora AI support?

+
Emora AI is available on iOS (iPhone), Android, and as a web application accessible via any modern browser on your desktop.

Does the app work offline?

+
You can still log your moods offline. Your data will securely sync to the cloud as soon as you regain an internet connection.

How can I contact customer support?

+
You can reach our support team via the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@emora.ai. We typically respond within 24 hours.
